# Resolution Steps
1.	Sign-In  to your respective Power Automate (meaning, commercial or government  cloud)
2.	Select Settings > View My Licenses
3.	Confirm Power Automate Premium appears
4.	Sign Out of:
1.	Power Automate Desktop
2.	Power Automate Web
5.	Restart the VDI Session
6.	Sign back into the Power Automate Desktop
